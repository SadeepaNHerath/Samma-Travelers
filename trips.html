Cultural Triangle Explorer

Trip Overview

Explore Sri Lanka's ancient cities and cultural heritage sites including UNESCO World Heritage locations. This 5-day journey takes you through the heart of Sri Lankan history and culture.

Highlights

  • Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress
  • Explore the ancient city of Anuradhapura
  • Visit the Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic in Kandy
  • Experience traditional cultural performances
  • Discover the cave temples of Dambulla

Trip Details

  • Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
  • Destinations: Anuradhapura, Sigiriya, Kandy
  • Group Size: 2-12 people
  • Availability: Year-round
  • Price: $599 per person
Book This Trip

Itinerary

Airport pickup and transfer to Anuradhapura. After check-in and refreshments, begin exploring the ancient city of Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Visit the sacred Sri Maha Bodhi tree, Ruwanwelisaya Stupa, and other historical monuments. Overnight in Anuradhapura.

After breakfast, travel to Sigiriya. Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, known for its frescoes and ancient engineering marvels. In the afternoon, visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, another UNESCO site featuring Buddhist mural paintings and statues. Overnight in Sigiriya.
