Our Story

Founded in 2010, Samma Travelers was born from a passion to share the authentic beauty of Sri Lanka with the world.

Our team consists of experienced local guides, knowledgeable drivers, and dedicated travel specialists who work together to create memorable experiences for our guests. With deep roots in Sri Lankan culture and extensive knowledge of the island's hidden gems, our team ensures that every journey with us is special.

We take pride in our commitment to sustainable tourism practices and supporting local communities throughout Sri Lanka.
